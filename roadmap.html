GRIT Roadmap

GRIT is starting as a lean web experience and growing into a full platform. Here’s the rough order of how that growth will unfold.

Phase 1 – Foundation

Now

  • Core website and content library (videos + articles)
  • Find a Therapist tool (guided matches, not a directory)
  • Three pillars: Physical, Mental, Spiritual

Phase 2 – Membership & Community

Next

  • Starter, Builder, and Warrior memberships
  • Weekly GRIT challenges and check-ins
  • Members-only Q&A and live sessions

Phase 3 – Mobile App

Planned

  • Native app experience for iOS and Android
  • Progress tracking, streaks, and habit systems
  • Offline access to core content

Phase 4 – Expanded Support

Future

  • Deeper collaborations with therapists and coaches
  • Workshops, retreats, and group intensives
  • Additional content for partners, spouses, and families

This roadmap will evolve as we listen, learn, and grow. If you have ideas or feedback for what GRIT should become, we’d love to hear it.
