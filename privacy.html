Privacy Policy

This page explains how GRIT approaches your information. Our goal is simple: be honest, respectful, and responsible.

What GRIT Is

GRIT is a platform focused on men’s physical, mental, and spiritual strength. It is not a medical provider, therapy service, or crisis response resource.

Information We May Collect

  • Basic usage data: pages visited, buttons clicked, time spent on the site
  • Technical data: browser type, device info, approximate location
  • Information you choose to share: messages sent through email or forms

GRIT does not intentionally collect highly sensitive medical or mental health records. Please be mindful about what personal information you share.

How Information Is Used

  • Improving site content and user experience
  • Understanding what tools and topics are helpful
  • Responding to questions or support requests

GRIT does not sell personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Tools

GRIT may use third-party services like analytics or email providers. These services may collect their own data according to their policies. GRIT will choose tools that align with a privacy-respectful approach.

Data Security

No online system is perfect, but reasonable measures will be taken to protect data from unauthorized access or misuse.

Children’s Privacy

GRIT is intended for adults and is not targeted at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may change as GRIT evolves. The “Last updated” date will be adjusted when it does.
