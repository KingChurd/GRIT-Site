Privacy Policy

Your data. Your control.

GRIT is built around trust and confidentiality. Below outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data needed to provide the GRIT experience, including:

  • Account details (name, email, login ID)
  • Usage data (videos watched, articles read, session activity)
  • Optional inputs (therapist form preferences, self-reflection answers)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize Mental, Physical, and Spiritual pillar suggestions
  • To improve content recommendations
  • To maintain account security and platform integrity

3. Therapist Finder Data

Your therapist search information is not stored or shared. It is processed only to generate your custom summary on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

GRIT may use secure third-party tools (such as payment processors) that operate under their own privacy policies.

5. Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to safeguard your account.

6. Your Choices & Rights

  • You may update or delete your data at any time.
  • You may request a full export of your account information.
  • You may unsubscribe from communications at any time.
