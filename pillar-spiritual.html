Pillar Three

Spiritual GRIT

Identity, purpose, and connection. However you define faith, this pillar is about who you’re becoming underneath the noise.

Start Here: Spiritual Basics

  • Take 3–5 minutes to be quiet, breathe, and ask, “Who am I becoming today?”
  • Write one thing you’re grateful for and one thing you want to do on purpose.
  • Reconnect weekly with whatever grounds you – prayer, scripture, nature, service.
