Who GRIT is for

GRIT is for men who are done pretending everything is fine, but also don’t want to live in constant crisis mode. Men who care about strength, responsibility, and integrity — even when life feels heavy or confusing.

  • Men balancing school, work, family, and faith
  • Men who feel tired of “all or nothing” approaches
  • Men who want character and competence, not just aesthetics
  • Men who prefer honesty and practicality over hype

What GRIT is — and what it isn’t

GRIT is a framework, a set of tools, and a collection of resources to help men grow. It’s here to support your physical, mental, and spiritual strength — not to replace therapy, medical care, or crisis services.

  • Not a licensed therapy or medical service
  • Not a crisis or emergency resource
  • Not about perfection or performance

If you are in crisis or thinking about harming yourself: call or text 988 in the U.S., contact your local emergency number (such as 911), or go to the nearest emergency room.
