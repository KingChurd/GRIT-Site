Questions you might be asking

Is GRIT a replacement for therapy?

No. GRIT is about building daily habits, strength, and resilience. It can support your growth alongside therapy, coaching, fitness, or spiritual practices, but it is not a licensed mental health service.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your membership at any time before your next billing period, and you’ll keep access until the end of your current cycle.

Which tier should I choose?

If you’re just starting, choose Starter. If you want structure and accountability, choose Builder. If you’re ready for deeper work and weekly coaching calls, choose Warrior.

When will the mobile app be available?

GRIT is launching as a web experience first. Your membership will carry over to the app once it’s live, and you’ll get updates as we roll out features.
