Don’t overcomplicate it.

Start Here with GRIT

GRIT is built for real men with real lives. You don’t need a perfect plan. You need a clear starting point and a next step you can actually do.

Step 1: Choose Your Pillar Focus

You can work on all three, but it helps to choose one to lean into first. Pick the pillar that feels most on fire right now:

Physical

Energy, strength, sleep, and feeling better in your body.

Mental

Stress, anxiety, burnout, and emotional overload.

Spiritual

Identity, faith, purpose, and who you’re becoming.

Step 2: Pick One Video and One Article

Don’t binge everything. Pick one short video and one article to start with.

  • Go to the GRIT Video Library
  • Browse featured GRIT articles

Step 3: Decide Your Baseline

Decide what “showing up” means for you this week. Make it so small it almost feels silly:

  • 7-minute movement session 3x this week
  • 5 minutes of grounding or prayer before bed
  • Write down one thing you’re proud of each day
